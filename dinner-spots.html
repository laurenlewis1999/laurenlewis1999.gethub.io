Dinner Spots

Del Seoul

Del Seouls

Del Seoul is a Korean BBQ Mexican fusion restraunt. They have a varitey of options ranging from Korean BBQ tacos to Kimchi French Fries. They also have more traditional Korean BBQ such as, Bibimbop and Bento Box Dinner plate options. If you are on the North Side of Chicago, you should stop by Del Seoul. Located at 2568 N. Clark St. Chicago, Illinois 60614.

Pasta Bowl

Pasta Bowls

Pasta Bowl is another great restaurant located on Clark. They have a variety of filling delicious pasta dishes as well as a large asourtment of wines. There's something for everyone here, rainbow pasta for the kids and for the pickest of eaters they are stepping boring old Mac and Cheese to the next level.
